# Exploratory Data Analysis Report

## Dataset
Student Performance Dataset

Total Records: 1000
Features: 8

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Score Correlation

Reading and Writing scores show a very strong correlation.
Math scores have a moderate correlation with both reading and writing.

---

### 2. Gender Performance

Female students perform slightly better in reading and writing.
Math performance is similar for both genders.

---

### 3. Test Preparation

Students who completed the test preparation course scored higher
in math, reading, and writing.

---

### 4. Lunch Impact

Students with standard lunch performed better than students
with free/reduced lunch.

---

### 5. Overall Observation

Academic performance is influenced by preparation,
parental education, and socioeconomic factors.

---

## Conclusion

The dataset shows that test preparation and
better socioeconomic conditions lead to improved
student academic performance.
